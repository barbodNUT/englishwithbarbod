1 Part 1: Multiple Choice

1. By the time the bus finally arrived, we ________ in the rain for nearly an hour.

Correct: had been waiting. Use Past Perfect Continuous for a duration leading up to a specific past point (the bus arrival).

2. I was exhausted because I ________ since 6:00 AM.

Correct: had been working. Explains the cause of a past state (being exhausted).

3. Look at your hands! They are filthy. ________ you ________ in the garden?

Correct: Have you been playing. Present Perfect Continuous shows the present result of a very recent activity.

4. She was disappointed because she ________ for the lead role for months, but someone else got it.

Correct: had been practicing. Emphasizes the duration of effort before a past event.

5. The ground was wet this morning; it ________ during the night.

Correct: had been raining. Past evidence of a continuous past activity.
